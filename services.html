Our Services

Structural Engineering Design

We provide comprehensive structural design solutions tailored for various industries including oil and gas, civil infrastructure, and renewable energy. Our designs prioritize safety, efficiency, and compliance with international standards.

  • Steel and concrete structural design
  • Load analysis and optimization
  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA) simulations
  • Structural integrity assessments

Mechanical Engineering Design

Our mechanical engineering services include the design and analysis of complex mechanical systems, equipment, and components critical to energy and infrastructure projects.

  • Equipment and piping design
  • Thermal and fluid systems analysis
  • Custom mechanical component development
  • Mechanical system optimization and troubleshooting

Simulation and Modeling

Utilizing advanced simulation tools, we deliver predictive models and digital prototypes that reduce risk, enhance performance, and improve project outcomes.

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Structural and thermal simulations
  • Dynamic and vibration analysis
  • Multiphysics modeling

Consulting and Project Management

We offer expert consulting services and project management support to ensure your projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

  • Feasibility studies and technical assessments
  • Project scheduling and resource planning
  • Regulatory compliance and risk management
  • Technical audits and quality assurance

Terminal Automation Systems

Specializing in automation solutions for oil and gas terminals, we design and integrate advanced control systems that optimize operational efficiency, improve safety, and ensure regulatory compliance.

  • SCADA system design and implementation
  • Real-time monitoring and data acquisition
  • Process automation and control optimization
  • Integration with existing infrastructure
